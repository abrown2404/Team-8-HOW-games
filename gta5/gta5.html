Grand Theft Auto V continues the groundbreaking open-world formula of the series, offering players the sprawling metropolis of Los Santos and its surrounding areas. The game features a narrative that follows three protagonists with interconnected stories, providing a vast and dynamic world filled with heists, activities, and a satirical take on modern American culture.
