The History of Open World Games

Elder Scrolls 3

Released on : May 1, 2002

The Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind is an open-world role-playing game set in the fantasy world of Tamriel. Players assume the role of the Nerevarine, a prophesied hero, and embark on a journey to save the land from a powerful threat. The game is celebrated for its vast, immersive world, rich lore, and the freedom it provides players to shape their own destinies through choices and actions. Its influence on subsequent open-world RPGs is evident in the freedom it grants players to shape their destinies.
