Banjo-Kazooie

Released on : June 29, 1998

Banjo-Kazooie takes the classic item-collecting formula from Super Mario 64 and pumps it full of steroids. Players must collect over a thousand items during their adventure to open all the twisting tunnels, polluted pipes, and tortuous passages that lead to the final confrontation with Gruntilda.
