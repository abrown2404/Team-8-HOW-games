The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

Released on :May 12, 2023

The Sequel to 2016's widely successful game Breath of the Wild, Tears of the Kingdom seeks and succeeds in improving every aspect of its predecesor. Now featuring both the sky islands above Hyrule and the cavernous depths below, Tears of the Kingdom is a truly massive Hyrule. Combinined with the revolutionary ability of Ultrahand, allowing players to create their own vehicles and contraptions to get across the land, Tears of the Kingdom revolutionzes the open world genre.
