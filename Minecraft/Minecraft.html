Minecraft

Released on : November 18, 2011

Minecraft needs no introduction. The best selling game of all time with 140 million active players more than a decade after release. Most of this success is attributed to the overwhelming creativity allowed to the player in Creative Mode, but let's not forget the achievements of Survival Mode. This mode essentially set the player in a procedurally generated open world, meaning every playthrough will be different, and just lets them go with no clear objective. This game is the logical end point of open level design.
