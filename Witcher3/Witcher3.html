The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

Released on : May 19, 2015

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt was developed with the goal of creating a complex story without compromising the open world. It succeeds in this area, with dialogue trees and multiple endings, complimented by a vibrant open world that rivals the best. The main character, Geralt of Rivia, is a renowned monster hunter searching for his kidnapped daughter.
