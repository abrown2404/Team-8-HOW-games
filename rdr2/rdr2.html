The History of Open World Games

Red Dead Redemption 2

Released on : October 26, 2018

Red Dead Redemption 2 continues the legacy of its predecessor, pushing the boundaries of open-world realism. Its attention to detail, expansive map, and the complex interactions within the open world set new standards, influencing the industry to prioritize depth and authenticity in open-world design.
