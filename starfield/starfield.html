Starfield

Released on : September 6th, 2023

Starfield is open-world space exploration game by Bethesda Game Studios. Set in an expansive and dynamic universe, the game promises to redefine the open-world genre by allowing players to chart their own course among the stars. With a focus on immersive storytelling, unique planets to explore, and the freedom to carve out one's destiny in the vastness of space, Starfield is poised to set a new standard for open-world space adventures.
