The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

Released on : March 3, 2017

Breath of The Wild was a huge depature from the traditional 3D Zelda games, and it payed off. It was directly inspired by the sheer openess of the first game in the series Featuring the largest Hyrule yet, the game sees its main character Link explore a ruined land 100 years after a great calamity. Players will venture from the cold Hebra region, to the scorching Eldin region. BOTW is one of the best games out on the Nintendo Switch, while also having its own sequel in Tears of the Kingdom.
