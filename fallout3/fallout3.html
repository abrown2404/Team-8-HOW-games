Fallout 3

Released on : October 28, 2008

Fallout 3 is a post-apocalyptic action role-playing game set in a retro-futuristic world devastated by nuclear war. Players emerge from an underground vault into the desolate wasteland of Washington, D.C., and embark on a quest to find their missing father. The game is renowned for its expansive open world, moral decision-making, and the iconic Vault Boy aesthetic.
