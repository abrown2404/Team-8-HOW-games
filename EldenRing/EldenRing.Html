Elden Ring

Released on : February 25, 2022

Elden Ring is one of the most fully realized open world games of all time, even winning game of the year in 2022. The Lands Between feels like a real world, with fully realized characters and lore. The sense of exploration is bolstered by the many collectables and bosses in the game. Although it may be extremly challenging, Elden Ring is a must play for any open world fan.
