The History of Open World Games

GTA 3

Released on : October 22, 2001

Grand Theft Auto III revolutionized the gaming industry by introducing the concept of an open-world, sandbox-style game. Players assume the role of a silent protagonist known as Claude as he navigates the criminal underworld of Liberty City. The game's non-linear gameplay and freedom to explore and undertake missions at will set a new standard for open-world gaming
